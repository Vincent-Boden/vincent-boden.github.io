TAAK 05: IT-autobiography:

Hello my name is Vincent Boden, a fanatic website builder. I’m 17 years old and I live in Beerse. My school is Immaculata Institute in Oostmalle where I study IT in my sixth year.

My first contact with IT was when I was very young around the age of six. One of my uncles worked in an IT-related business and every time I saw him he told something about what they did. Most of all they made websites and I was really interested even when I was that young. When my parents bought my first pc my addiction started to grow. First I just started with playing games and visiting info websites about stores in my city but when I was older I started to learn how to use my computer as a software development tool with the help of my uncle when he had time. Of course I still play lots of games in my spare time but building websites is also something that I like.

IT means a lot for me, every single moment when I didn’t plan anything that day I take my computer and I play games, build websites, do something for school, … . There is actually not a single day that I didn’t touched my pc. In the vacations I use my pc even more because on my holiday work I work 9 hours a day with a pc and when I come home from working I start up my own pc. Sometimes they even ask IT-related questions at me on my holiday work and it gives a really nice feeling if I can help them with what I know. So I can’t actually say how much time I spend on my computer a day because it’s always different. I am not an expert in what I do but I evolved a lot with my IT skills. If I look at what I could three years ago and what I can do know … . Three years ago I only knew the basics and know every day on school I learn something new about computers.

I think when I finished my sixth year on Immaculate Institute I am going to study something with building websites in Antwerp. Maybe in the course of this school year I am going to follow a few lessons about JavaScript, HTML and CSS at Karel de Grote high school. If I pass that exam I don’t have to study those lessons anymore next year and the things I learn there could always come handy this school year. After I finished my bachelor or master I hope I can start of course in a business where they make beautiful, interactive and responsive websites so I can fully express my passion into my work.

IT is as I already mentioned something really important in my life. I can’t imagen a life without my computer, smartphone, … . It is a big part of my life and I really like it that my friends of the class share this passion with me so there is always something IT-related to talk about in the class.

1 Jan 2015
